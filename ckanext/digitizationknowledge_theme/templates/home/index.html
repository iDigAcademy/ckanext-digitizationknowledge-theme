Our Approach – A Strategy Built for You.

Digitization knowledge provides a comprehensive knowledge base of digitization resources. We bring together resources created by the community and provide powerful tools to make them easily discoverable and accessible to all.

Digitization Knowledge is built upon three core components:

Aggregate

We bring together a collection of essential resources to support your digitization work.

Enrich

We enhance resource discovery by adding tailored metadata, making relevant materials easier and faster to find.

Engage

We foster a collaborative environment for discussing resources so that we can learn from each other's experiences.
